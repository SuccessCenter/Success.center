Course Description

A professional program designed to provide nurses with advanced skills and knowledge in specialized areas such as neonatal intensive care or nursing service management.

Study Options & Schedule

Objectives of the Nursing Training Diploma

Skill Development

Gain advanced skills in specialized nursing fields such as nephrology or neonatal care.

Quality Improvement

Enhance professional performance to deliver better patient care.

Leadership Preparation

Develop managerial skills for leading nursing teams and healthcare facilities.

Qualification Advancement

Supports career progression from Practical Nurse to Registered Nurse (RN).
